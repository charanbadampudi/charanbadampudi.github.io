About

I'm Charan Badampudi, a Computer Science graduate from Parul University with a focused interest in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, and Data Analytics. I enjoy the entire data lifecycle — from wrangling messy datasets and uncovering patterns through exploratory analysis to building predictive models and deploying them as usable applications.

My project work spans multiple AI domains: I've built an audio enhancement engine using DSP and ML, an NLP-based resume matching platform, and a vision chatbot powered by YOLOv8, BLIP, and BERT. These projects taught me that good AI isn't just about model accuracy — it's about understanding the data, asking the right questions, and building systems people can actually interact with.

During my internship at Aenexz Tech in Bangalore, I worked hands-on with real datasets — cleaning, analyzing, visualizing, and building predictive models. That experience deepened my appreciation for the analytical side of data work: finding stories in numbers, building dashboards, and translating business questions into data-driven answers.

I'm equally passionate about the data analytics pipeline — writing SQL queries, building visualizations, performing statistical analysis — as I am about training deep learning models. I believe the best ML engineers are also strong analysts.
